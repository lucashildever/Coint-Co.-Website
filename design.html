The new
Lydian Coin

“the coin brings the balance between history and modernity”

Redesigning one of the most classic and important coins in human history was a great challenge and Coint Co is proud to present the “New Lydian Coin” to everyone. The coin was designed by Renato Saes, known for impressing with innovative projects, publications and design awards.

According to the designer, the coin brings the balance between history and modernity in addition to being an innovative project. It is the first coin to present real value in cryptocurrency, it should be a project thought out in the smallest details.

With defined and striking lines and the harmony between the surfaces, the finishes give a special touch to this launch.
